Program Overview

Empowering Lives, Transforming Communities

Our Vision

  • To reach the poor and distressed with the good news.

Mission

  • We empower orphans and rural women facing discrimination through income-generating initiatives, formal education, and basic technical/vocational skills.

Core Values

  • Transparency: We strive to build a culture of transparency and demand the same in all our interactions and networking.
  • Accountability: We ensure and affirm accountability in all our activities to build a better and prosperous Sierra Leone.
  • Respect: We respect the dignity of every human person, including children and all persons we work for and within all our interventions.
  • Gender Equality: We are gender-sensitive, striving to achieve gender equality in all our programs and activities.

Objectives of the Organization

  • To provide a clean and healthy condition in the correctional center environment.
  • To provide a home and parents for orphans.
  • To create job opportunities and food for women.
  • To take the Gospel to the communities for salvation.
  • To help the less privileged with educational opportunities.

Short info

GARD SL, a Sierra Leone NGO since 2020, empowers orphans and rural women through income projects and education, fostering clean environments and homes while promoting community development through Gospel outreach.
